Advantages and disadvantages of using Fireworks

  1. Advantages
  • We can use the pentool to draw/sketch out our idea
  • We can easily edit since it has different layers
  1. Disadvantages
    It is harder to edit after you export and keep the changes

Advantages and disadvantages of using Dreamweaver

  1. Advantages
  • It is easier to edit after you finished
  1. Disadvantages
  • It is more challenging than fireworks
